Information

  • Each recipe will contain at most 1 solid ingredient. Recipes will contain either 3 liquids, or 2 liquids and 1 solid.

  • Ingredient #2 in each recipe is the limiting reactant, or the one consumed when the ingredients are combined. To produce a large amount of LC or AP, the #2 ingredient should be obtained in bulk, with the other two ingredients added in smaller amounts.

  • 'RCPU' is referred to internally in the game as 'probability', but doesn't reflect the accuracy of the calculated formula. This ONLY affects the rate at which the reaction occurs when the ingredients are combined.

  • Mod for calculating recipes in-game can be found here: modworkshop.net/mod/29782.If you encounter any errors with formula calculation, feel free to message me on Discord at Neff#6398.
